# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Primary: Potential freelance clients — founders, product managers, and CTOs at startups and small companies who need a reliable technical partner for web product work. They're reviewing portfolios quickly, deciding whether to reach out. Secondary: Recruiters and engineering leads at tech companies.

## Product Purpose

A personal portfolio that demonstrates craft, technical depth, and professional maturity. Success means a visitor closes the page wanting to reach out — not because of flashy effects, but because they trust the person behind the work.

## Brand Personality

Precise, human, confident. The voice of someone who has done this for 17+ years and doesn't need to prove it loudly. The design should whisper quality, not shout features.

## Anti-references

- Generic AI dev portfolio: gradient text names, typewriter effects, stats (50+ projects / 100% satisfaction), floating code decorations, identical skill icon cards.
- Agency/studio spectacular: elaborate scroll animations, 3D cursor effects, demo-reel feel that distracts from the actual work.
- Generic freelancer layout: hero → skills → timeline → contact, completely forgettable.
- SaaS landing page: headline + subheadline + CTA + feature grid — sells a product, not a person.

## Design Principles

1. **Work speaks for itself.** The projects are the product. Everything else is scaffolding to get the visitor to the work.
2. **Restraint is confidence.** What you leave out communicates as much as what you include. No element should be decorative without intent.
3. **Human before professional.** A real person with real experience — not a resume printed on a webpage. The copy and image choices should feel personal.
4. **Precision in every detail.** Typography, spacing, and motion should feel considered, not generated. The craft of the portfolio mirrors the craft of the work.
5. **Silence earns attention.** Space and stillness let the important things be seen.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Support for reduced motion via `prefers-reduced-motion`. Light/dark theme with sufficient contrast on both modes. Portuguese (BR) + English bilingual via existing i18n system.
